About the Project

The 5S Principles

  • Sort: Remove unnecessary items.
  • Set in Order: Designate homes for essentials.
  • Shine: Keep your environment clean.
  • Standardize: Build consistent habits.
  • Sustain: Commit to continuous improvement.
